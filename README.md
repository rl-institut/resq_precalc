Introduction
============

This repository contains a couple of precalculation scripts in the ResQEnergy project:

1. COP of the industrial heat waste (under construction): `calc_cop_per_industrial_waste_heat.py`
2. Calculation of the mean temperature of the BTB-dh network: `calc_inlet_temperature_BTB.py`
3. Calculation of the total heat quantity of the Teltow Canal at the measuring point: `calc_waermemenge_teltowkanal.py`
4. Calculation of a mean electricity price from a time series: `get_mean_el_price.py`
5. Generation of a plot showing the shares of supply in future district heating systems according to Langfristszenarien: `get_plot_share_production_dh.py`
6. A helping script to modify oemof-B3 weatherdata with `Testreferenzjahre`-ts: `modify_oemof-B3_weatherdata.py`
